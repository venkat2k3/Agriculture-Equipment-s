KHARIFCROPS

  • They grown in Rainy Season

  • Rainy season in India is from June to September

  • These crops require a substantial amount of water for their growth Kharif crops are harvested at the end of the monsoon season during September or October

  • These crops are also known as Paddy crops.


  • Some crops like Rice, Corn, Soyabean, Sugarcane, Groundnut, Cotton which were grown in the summer season.
