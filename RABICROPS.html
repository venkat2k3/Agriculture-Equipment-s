RABI-CROPS

1. RABI-Crops are summer season crops with High rainfall.

2. Wet and dry weather regions and well-suited for these Rabi Crops.

3. Mostly the crops are harvested from August to December.

4. They are also known as gap-fillers Middle crops.


Some crops like Cucumber, pumpkin, tomato, bitter gourd, watermelon which were grown in the summer season.
