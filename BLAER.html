BALER

ADVANTAGES OF BLAER ?

1.Low capital investment.
2.Flexible harvest and feedout.
3.Quality as good as bunker.
4.Low DM losses (<5-10%).
5.Low storage costs.
6.Low pollution risk.
7.Can be used in TMRs.
8.Can sell surplus.

DISADVANTAGES OF BLAER ?

1.More expensive to make
2.Unsuitable for low DM
3.More variability
4.Prone to damage
5.Labour/time at feedout
6.Plastic displosal
