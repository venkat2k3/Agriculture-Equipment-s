UREA
CROPS : ALL CROPS

UREA (46% N) Fertilizers provide three primary nutrients: Nitrogen (N), Phosphorus (P) and Potassium (K).

ADVANTAGES OF UREA ?

1.Low production cost, as source is natural.
2.Non-flammable and risk-free storage.
3.Wide application range, for all types of crops and soils.
4.Neutral pH and harmless to crops and soil.

DISADVANTAGES OF UREA ?

1.Excessive use of urea can easily damage the fertilizer.
2.Urea can only be used after it has been kept at room temperature for 4-5 days.
3.Urea can cause inflammation of the skin, eyes, and respiratory tract.
