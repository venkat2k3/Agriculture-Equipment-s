TRASHING

ADVANTAGES OF TRASHING ?

1.The mechanical harvesting machines were helpful for clearing large tracts of land.
2.Breaking up soil, removing grass and preparing the ground for cultivation in a short span of time and with less human labour.

DISADVANTAGES OF TRASHING ?

1. Tree injury, root damage,debris and trash.
2.The need for specific orchard design and tree training.
3.significant capital investment, and machine maintenance costs.
