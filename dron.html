ADVANTAGES OF DRONE SPRAYER ?

1.In agriculture, a crop sprayer is a piece of equipment that uses spray nozzles to apply controlled doses of herbicides, pesticides, and fertilizers to agricultural crops. 2.It is used for controlling pests. Pesticides are mixed with water and then sprayed to areas infested.

DISADVANTAGES OF DRON SPRAYER ?

1. The spray should take about two minutes to press the solution into the spray before spraying.
2. The use method is different from other sprayers. You need to understand the manual before you can use it.
3.Heavy, noisy, polluted, high temperature and poor operating environment.
