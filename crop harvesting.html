RICE-HARVESTER

ADVANTAGE OF RICE CROP-HARVERSTER ?

1.Combine harvester provides cleaner grains by effectively
weeds from grain.
2.The farm can be prepared easily in shorter time for the next crop.
3.Farmers can save the overall cost of harvesting from cutting to winnowing.
4.It reduces dependency on the human labour.

DISADVANTAGES OF RICE CROP-HARVESTER ?

1.The disadvantages of rice harvesters included bulky and heavy
which requires better-constructed infrastructures as roads and pathways to move to the fields.
2.The prices of harvesters were high.
3.The rice reapers needed extra labors for rice gathering after cutting.
