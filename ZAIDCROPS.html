ZAID CROPS

1. ZAID Crops are summer season crops with low rainfall.

2. Warm and dry weather regions and well-suited for these Zaid Crops.

3. Mostly the crops are harvested from March to June.

4.They are also known as gap-fillers between the two main crops.



Some crops like Cucumber, pumpkin, tomato, bitter gourd, watermelon which were grown in the summer season.
