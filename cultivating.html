CULTIVATING TRACTOR

ADVANTAGES OF CULTIVATING TRACTORS?

1.Has multiple uses.
2.More precise work.and Saves time and effort.
3.Suitable for small and large farmlands.and Cost-efficient.

DISADVANTAGES OF CULTIVATING TRACTORS ?

1.Tractors are not as fast as other types of farm machinery like combines, planters and harvesters.
2.They require more fuel to operate.
3.Tractors use large amounts of land for cultivation.
4.They need to be driven by a person.
