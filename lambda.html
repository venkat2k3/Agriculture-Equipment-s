Lambda-Cyhalothrin
CROPS : SEASONAL CROPS

INSECTS : ALL-INSECTS

1.Lambda-Cyhalothrin has the ability to kill target insects
either through coming into physical contact
with the chemical or via consumption.
2.Lambda-Cyhalothrin is a preferred active ingredient for many pest control
product manufacturers due to its long-lasting residual
effect compared to other chemical ingredients.

lambda-cyhalothrin harmful to humans?

1.Exposure to Lambda-cyhalothrin poses both acute and chronic risks.
2.Acute effects include skin and eye irritation,
coma, convulsions and severe muscle fasciculation.
