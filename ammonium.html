Ammonium
NAME: AMMONIUM-NITRATE

FORMULA: ammonia N (N-NH4) - 13.5 %

CROPS: Available To All Crops,Except Rice

ADVANTAGES OF AMMONIUM-NITRATE ?

1.The very high solubility of ammonium nitrate makes
it well suited for making solutions for fertigation or foliar sprays.
2.Easy handling and high nutrient content make ammonium
nitrate a popular N fertilizer
3.It's also very soluble in the soil, and the nitrate portion
can move beyond the root zone under wet conditions.
