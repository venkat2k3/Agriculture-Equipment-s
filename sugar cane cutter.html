SUGAR CANE CUTTER

ADVANTAGES OF SUGAR CANE CUTTER ?

1.It cuts the stalks at the base.
2.The harvester used to cut the strips leaves.
Then cut the cane into segments.
3.It removes the dust and dirt particles.
4.The sugarcane harvester ejected water
material onto the field, where it acts as a fertilizer.

DISADVANTAGES OF SUGAR CANE CUTTER ?

1.Machines are expensive to buy, maintain and repair.
2.Machine with or without uninterrupted use will get broken and worn-out.
