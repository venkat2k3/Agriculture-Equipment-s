LANNATE

USES :
1.Glyphosate is one of the most widely used herbicides with applications :
agriculture, forestry, industrial weed control, lawn,
garden, and aquatic environments. 2.Sites with the largest lannate use
include soybeans, field corn, pasture and hay.

Why is it an advantage to make plants resistant to lannate?

1.This lannate resistance enables farmers to wipe out most
weeds from the fields without damaging their crops.
2.lannate inhibits plant growth by blocking an enzyme
known as EPSP synthase, Roundup, can trigger loss of biodiversity,
3.making ecosystems more vulnerable to pollution and climate change,
lannate may cause eye or skin irritation.
