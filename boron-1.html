BORON

NAME : BORON
TYPE: SOLID
CROPS : ALL FRUIT PLANTS

ADVANTAGES OF BORON ?

1.One of the essential nutrients for the optimum growth,
development, yield, and quality of crops

DISADVANTAGES OF BORON ?

1.excess boron can cause bark to crack or become corky.
2.Doses over 20 mg daily might cause male fertility problems.
3.Large doses can also cause poisoning.
