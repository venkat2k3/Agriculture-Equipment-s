NPK

NAME : NPK

CROPS : all crops

ADVANTAGES OF NPK ?

1.Balances the soil ecosystem.
2.Boosts plant health naturall.
3.The process of decomposition requires no chemical intervention

DISADVANTAGES OF NPK ?

1.NPK directly affects plant growth by feeding the plant.
2.Some organics can take longer to green up the lawn.
3.It often takes longer to help your plants grow.
